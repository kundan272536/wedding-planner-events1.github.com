    • How much do wedding planners cost?

      The cost depends on the responsibilities you want your wedding planner to undertake, the experience of the professional, services and other factors.

    • Why are wedding planners important?

      Desire to make your most important day perfect has brought them into the mainstream. From destination weddings to theme weddings, all weddings are unique and it is the wedding planner who makes these weddings flawless with intricate planning and execution. Most importantly, they save you a lot of hassle and help you to enjoy the event properly without worrying about every little arrangement.

    • What are some things to keep in mind before booking a Wedding Planner?

      Before booking a wedding planner it is essential that you understand their role, decide your budget and then choose one as per their expertise. Do keep another one or two wedding planners in mind as the one you want to engage may not be free. Make a list of tasks you want them to take care of like wedding decorations, food, guests accommodation, etc. Discuss everything in detail to get the ball rolling.
